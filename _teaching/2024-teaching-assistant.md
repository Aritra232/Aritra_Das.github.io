I served as a Teaching Assistant at East West University, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, for a tenure of two years. During this period, I supported faculty members in delivering computer science courses and provided academic guidance to undergraduate and graduate students. My responsibilities included conducting tutorial sessions, assisting with course material development, grading assignments and examinations, and helping students understand complex programming and AI concepts. This experience enhanced my communication skills and deepened my understanding of foundational computer science principles. I also conducted workshops on machine learning and deep learning topics, helping students bridge the gap between theory and practical applications in emerging AI technologies.

**Position: January 2024 - December 2025**
