Welcome
======
I'm Aritra Das, a Junior AI Developer at SM Technology with a passion for Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Computer Vision. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from East West University (CGPA: 3.76) and am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in CSE (CGPA: 3.91).

About Me
======
I am a dedicated AI developer and researcher focused on creating intelligent solutions that solve real-world problems. With a strong foundation in computer science and hands-on experience in machine learning projects, I have developed expertise in building scalable AI systems and conducting cutting-edge research. I am passionate about exploring emerging technologies in deep learning and AI, particularly in areas like natural language processing and computer vision. My goal is to contribute meaningfully to the field of artificial intelligence while continuously learning and adapting to new challenges. I believe in the power of collaborative research and open-source contributions to advance the AI community.

Research Interests
======
My research interests span across multiple domains within artificial intelligence and machine learning. I am particularly drawn to Large Language Models and their applications in solving multilingual NLP challenges, especially for low-resource languages like Bengali. I am passionate about developing computer vision systems for real-world applications such as traffic monitoring and safety compliance. Additionally, I am interested in exploring self-supervised learning techniques and Graph Neural Networks for novel applications. I also focus on building efficient vector databases and RAG pipelines that can enhance the capabilities of modern AI systems. Through my research, I aim to bridge the gap between academic innovation and practical industry solutions.
