Welcome
======
I'm **Aritra Das**, a Junior AI Developer at **SM Technology** with a passion for **Machine Learning**, **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**, and **Computer Vision**. I hold a **B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering** from East West University (CGPA: 3.76) and am currently pursuing an **M.Sc. in CSE** (CGPA: 3.91).

About Me
======
I specialize in developing AI-powered solutions and conducting research in deep learning. My expertise spans:

- **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and prompt engineering
- **Natural Language Processing** for multilingual applications
- **Computer Vision** and object detection systems
- **Deep Learning** frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow)
- **Vector Databases** and RAG pipelines
- **Graph Neural Networks (GNN)** and self-supervised learning

Experience & Projects
======
With experience as a **Teaching Assistant** at East West University and an **ML Internship** at ACME AI, I've contributed to various research projects including:

- AI-powered recommendation chatbots using LLMs
- Traffic flow prediction and vehicle detection systems
- Real-time safety monitoring with computer vision
- Flower growth stage classification using GNN models
- Bangla-English sentiment and emotion analysis systems

I've published research papers in reputable journals and won the **Best Paper Award** at the 5th International Conference on Applied Intelligence and Informatics (AII 2025) for work on code-mixed emotion detection in e-commerce reviews.
