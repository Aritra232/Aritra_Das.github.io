## Welcome

I'm **Aritra Das**, a Junior AI Developer at **SM Technology** with a passion for Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Computer Vision. I hold a **B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering** from East West University (CGPA: 3.76) and am currently pursuing an **M.Sc. in CSE** (CGPA: 3.91).

### About Me

I specialize in developing AI-powered solutions and conducting research in deep learning, with expertise in:
- **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and prompt engineering
- **Natural Language Processing** for multilingual applications
- **Computer Vision** and object detection systems
- **Deep Learning** frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow)
- **Vector Databases** and RAG pipelines

### Experience & Projects

With experience as a Teaching Assistant at East West University and an ML internship at ACME AI, I've contributed to various research projects including traffic flow prediction using YOLO, real-time safety detection systems, and AI-powered recommendation chatbots. I've also published research papers in reputable journals and won the **Best Paper Award at the 5th International Conference on Applied Intelligence and Informatics (AII 2025)** held in Washington, D.C.

This website showcases my **publications, projects, talks, and academic contributions**. Feel free to explore my work and get in touch!
